Outdated Pages

These are static websites hosted using GitHub Pages. Your browser saves webpages in its cache, and it may show the cached page even if the website was changed recently. As far as I know, GitHub Pages doesn't allow me to tell browsers to always check for a more up to date version of this website, so you may see the old version after I've updated it.

One solution is to clear your browser's cache before checking these websites if you expect to see new material. Another option is to block my GitHub Pages websites from your browser's cache:

  • Firefox
    • Open Settings and click "Privacy & Security"
    • Scroll down to "Cookies and Site Data"
    • Click "Manage Exceptions..."
    • Add "https://zachkohlberg.github.io" and "http://zachkohlberg.github.io" to the list of websites and click "Save Changes"
  • Chrome
    • Click the icon to the left of the URL (tooltip should read "View site information")
    • Click "Cookies and site data"
    • Click "Manage on-device site data"
    • Click the three dots to the right of the URL
    • Select "Don't allow to save data"
    • Click "Done"
  • If you use another browser, then you may need to look up instructions for preventing specific websites from caching pages with that browser

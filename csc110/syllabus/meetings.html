CSC110 Syllabus

Class Meetings

Instructor Absence

I may hold class remotely via Zoom while sick or otherwise unable to attend in person. If I need to cancel class, then I will notify everyone as early as possible in a Blackboard announcement.

Class Structure

Class meetings will be a mix of lectures, practice exercises, and live coding. The course schedule will list the topics we're covering in a given week as well as any planned activities for a class outside of that day's lecture. Material prepared ahead of time, such as practice exercises and lecture outlines, will be posted online before class. Programs coded during class will also be posted after the meeting.

Attendance Policy

I don't grade you on attendance. You're free to miss class whenever you decide it's prudent or necessary to do so, and you don't need to give me an excuse. However, you are still responsible for all material and work that you missed.

If you miss a class, you should:

  • Check the course schedule to see what we did that day
  • Complete any activities you missed
  • Review any new material that was covered with a classmate who attended the meeting
  • Review any notes or other material posted after class

If you are feeling sick or suspect you may have COVID, the flu, or another contagious illness, then please stay home and focus on recovering. You're not likely to get much out of class while you aren't feeling well, and attending class while sick also poses a risk to everyone else. If you want to attend while you suspect you have a contagious illness, then wear a mask.
